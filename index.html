Vegan Origin Stories

Are you vegan for the animals?

Want to share your vegan origin story?

Vegan origin stories are powerful because they offer personal narratives that resonate with individuals on an emotional level. By sharing these stories, non-vegans can relate to others which can help them overcome their own cognitive dissonance and become vegan!

Instructions

1. Find a quiet place with a neutral background
2. Hold your phone in potrait mode
3. In 60 seconds or less, share your vegan origin story
4. By submitting your video, you agree to have your story used on the Vegan Origin Stories social media accounts (YouTube, Instagram and TikTok)
